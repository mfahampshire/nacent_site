From September to November 2020 we hosted a chat-only reading and writing group (broadly) investigating alternate temporalities, capitalism, computation, and cryptography. In this first iteration we focussed on Anna Greenspan's PhD thesis, 'Capitalism's Transcendental Time Machine'.

Week 1: Introduction + 'Philosophy's Copernican Revolution: Time in The Critique of Pure Reason'
Week 2: 'Time in Modern Capitalism'
Week 3: 'The Materialist Revolution: Time in Capitalism and Schizophrenia' + (optionally) 'Deleuze's Third Synthesis of Time' by Daniela Voss
Week 4: 'The Aeonic Occurrence: Y2K' + conclusion

It took place in this urbit channel: ~sovnub-banleg/temporal-secessionism.
